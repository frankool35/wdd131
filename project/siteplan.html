Kpec Schools Website Plan

Site Name

Kpec Schools - This name represents the school identity and serves as the official hub for students, parents, and staff. It was chosen to ensure clarity and easy recognition of the institution.

Optional domain availability: kpecschools.org

Site Purpose

The website provides information about the school's history, academics, and admissions process. It also acts as a communication hub with announcements, events, and a contact page for inquiries.

Scenarios

  • What are the admission requirements and how do I apply?
  • Where can I find the school calendar and upcoming events?
  • What programs and extracurricular activities are available?
