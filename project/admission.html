1. Overview

Welcome to Kpec Schools, where we nurture excellence, discipline, and creativity from the foundation level to higher classes. Admission into our Primary and Secondary sections is open to all pupils and students who meet our age and academic requirements.

We provide a safe, stimulating learning environment, experienced teachers, and a rich curriculum that builds confident and capable learners.

2. Admission Requirements

A. Primary Section (Nursery 1 - Basic 5)

Eligibility:

  • For children aged 3 to 11 years (depending on class level).
  • Pupils transferring from other schools must have completed their previous class in a recognized institution.

Required Documents:

  • Completed Admission Form (available online or at the school office)
  • Birth Certificate or Age Declaration
  • Two (2) recent passport photographs
  • Previous school report card or transfer certificate (if applicable)
  • Medical fitness report from a recognized hospital

Placement Test:

Applicants will take a short assessment test to determine the right class placement.

Eligibility:

  • Must have completed Primary 6 or its equivalent.
  • Transfer students must present academic records from their previous school.

Required Documents:

  • Completed Admission Form
  • Birth Certificate or Age Declaration
  • Two (2) recent passport photographs
  • Last school result or testimonial
  • Medical fitness certificate

Entrance Examination:

All applicants will take an entrance exam covering:

  • English Language
  • Mathematics
  • Basic Science or General Paper

Successful candidates will be invited for an interview and orientation.

3. How to Apply

Option 1: Online Application

  1. Visit our website: www.kpecschools.com/admission
  2. Fill out the online admission form and upload required documents.
  3. Submit and await a confirmation email or SMS.

Option 2: In-Person Application

  1. Obtain the admission form from the School Administrative Office.
  2. Complete the form and attach the necessary documents.
  3. Submit the form to the admission officer.

4. Admission Timeline

  • Sales of Admission Forms: Ongoing throughout the year
  • Entrance Examination: Conducted every term before resumption
  • Interview & Orientation: Dates communicated individually
  • Resumption: As stated on the school calendar
