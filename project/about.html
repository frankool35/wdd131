About Kpec Schools

Our Mission

To build a world class institute that will present Nigeria's image to the outside world, as a country with vision, culture and well-grounded morals.

Our Values

Excellence, Tolerance, Creativity, Diligence, Service

History

K-PEC started as an illusion. The illusion culminated and metamorphosed into reality in the year 2002, January 5th, with the establishment of K-PEC Tutorial College, Ketu - Lagos.

In furtherance of the mission and vision, K-PEC High School was established in the year 2009, September 16th along 64, Demurin Road, Ketu-Lagos.

In pursuit of the mission and vision the school moved to its present site, 77, Agboyi Road, Alapere Ketu-Lagos in the year 2012, January.

K-PEC operates as a co-educational British and Nigerian National curriculum, independent and pacesetter school in a conducive environment set on a beautiful ground within Agboyi - Alapere, Ketu-Lagos in Nigeria.

More so, an off-campus dormitory has been organised to cater for the needs of intending boarders. There are varieties of well-maintained facilities that create an environment for teaching and learning.
